LeBron Raymone James Sr., born December 30, 1984, is an American professional basketball player for the Los Angeles Lakers of the National Basketball Association (NBA). Often compared to Michael Jordan for the title of the greatest basketball player of all time, his accomplishments include three NBA championships, four NBA Most Valuable Player Awards, three NBA Finals MVP Awards, and two Olympic gold medals. James has appeared in fifteen NBA All-Star Games and been named NBA All-Star MVP three times. He won the 2008 NBA scoring title, is the all-time NBA playoffs scoring leader, is fourth in all-time career points scored, and is ninth in all-time assists. He has been voted onto the All-NBA First Team twelve times and the All-Defensive First Team five times.
